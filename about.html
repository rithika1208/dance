About Us

Pulse Dance Company

Founded in 2010, Pulse Dance Company began as a small group of passionate artists with a dream to create a space where dancers could grow, express, and perform without limits. Over the years, we have evolved into one of New York’s most respected dance academies, known for blending technical excellence with creative storytelling.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission: To inspire dancers of all backgrounds by providing world-class training, creative opportunities, and a supportive artistic community.

Vision: To become a global hub for dance education, performance, and innovation.

  • Excellence in training and performance
  • Creativity, diversity, and inclusion
  • Integrity, discipline, and teamwork

Why Choose Us

What Makes Pulse Dance Company Different

At Pulse Dance Company, we go beyond teaching steps — we build confident performers and passionate artists. Our students choose us for our commitment to quality, creativity, and real-world dance exposure.

  • Industry-experienced instructors and choreographers
  • Professional-level studios with performance-ready training
  • Career guidance, auditions prep & portfolio building
  • Inclusive, supportive, and motivating dance community

Our Team

Founders, Instructors & Choreographers

Pulse Dance Company was founded by industry veterans who have performed and choreographed for international stages, television, and award shows. Today, our team includes master instructors, creative directors, and mentors dedicated to shaping confident performers.
