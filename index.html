Welcome to Unity Dance Company

Your Destination for Dance & Fitness

At Unity Dance Company, we believe dance and fitness should be accessible, enjoyable, and inspiring for everyone. Our studio offers a wide variety of dance styles including Western, Bollywood, Folk, Hip Hop, Freestyle, and Contemporary dance. Along with dance training, we provide Zumba and fitness programs for boys and girls, helping students stay active, confident, and healthy.

Dance Styles

Western, Bollywood, Folk, Hip Hop, Freestyle, and Contemporary dance styles taught by experienced instructors.

Zumba & Fitness

Zumba Fitness, Aerobics, Stick Workouts, Chair Workouts, Ball Workouts, Leg Workouts, and more — available for boys and girls.

Why Choose Us

Friendly trainers, flexible batches, multiple styles, and a positive environment that motivates every student to perform confidently.
