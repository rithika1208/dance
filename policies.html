Dance Studio Policies

Studio Policies & Guidelines

At Unity Dance Company, our studio policies are designed to ensure a safe, professional, and welcoming environment for all students. These guidelines help maintain discipline, fairness, and quality training across all dance classes.

Terms & Conditions

By enrolling in any dance class, workshop, or program at Unity Dance Company, students and parents agree to follow all studio rules, schedules, fee structures, and instructor guidelines. Enrollment confirms acceptance of studio timings, class structure, and behavioral expectations.

Privacy Policy

Unity Dance Company values your privacy. Personal information collected during registration, including names, contact details, and age, is used only for communication, scheduling, and studio management. We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties without prior consent.

Refund, Cancellation & Attendance Policy

  • • All dance class fees are non-refundable once the course or session has started.
  • • Missed classes are not eligible for refunds and may be rescheduled only if slots are available.
  • • Class cancellations must be informed at least 24 hours in advance.
  • • Regular attendance and punctuality are expected to ensure proper learning progress.

Code of Conduct

Unity Dance Company maintains a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free environment. Any form of bullying, discrimination, or inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated. The studio reserves the right to take disciplinary action if policies are violated.

  • • Show respect to instructors, staff, and fellow dancers at all times.
  • • Follow safety instructions and studio rules during every class.
  • • Handle studio property and equipment responsibly.
  • • Help create a positive and motivating dance learning environment.
