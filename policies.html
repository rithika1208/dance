Dance Studio Policies

Studio Policies & Guidelines

  • Ensure a safe, professional, and welcoming environment
  • Maintain discipline, fairness, and transparency
  • Provide high-quality training for all students
  • Promote respect, inclusivity, and responsibility

Terms & Conditions

  • Enrollment confirms acceptance of all studio rules and policies
  • Students must follow instructor guidance and safety instructions
  • Class schedules and timings must be strictly followed
  • Fees and program structure are agreed upon at the time of registration

Privacy Policy

  • Personal information is collected only for studio operations
  • Data is used for communication, scheduling, and administration
  • Student details are kept confidential and secure
  • No personal data is shared with third parties without consent

Refund, Cancellation & Attendance Policy

  • All class fees are non-refundable once the session begins
  • Missed classes are not eligible for refunds
  • Make-up classes are subject to availability
  • Cancellations must be informed at least 24 hours in advance
  • Regular attendance and punctuality are mandatory

Code of Conduct

  • Maintain respectful and professional behavior at all times
  • Zero tolerance for bullying, harassment, or discrimination
  • Follow studio rules and safety guidelines strictly
  • Use studio facilities and equipment responsibly
  • Support a positive and inclusive learning environment
