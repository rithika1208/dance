Policies

Studio Policies & Guidelines

At Pulse Dance Company, we believe in creating a safe, respectful, and professional environment for every dancer. Please review our policies below to understand your rights and responsibilities.

Terms & Conditions

By enrolling in any class or program at Pulse Dance Company, students agree to follow studio rules, safety guidelines, and instructor directions. Enrollment confirms acceptance of schedules, fees, and studio policies.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. All personal information collected during registration is kept confidential and used only for communication, scheduling, and studio operations. We never share your data with third parties without consent.

Refund, Cancellation & Attendance

  • • All class fees are non-refundable once the session begins.
  • • Missed classes cannot be refunded but may be rescheduled based on availability.
  • • Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance.
  • • Students are expected to arrive on time and attend regularly.

Code of Conduct

Pulse Dance Company maintains a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, bullying, or discrimination. All dancers, parents, and staff must treat one another with respect and professionalism at all times.

  • • Maintain respectful behavior inside and outside the studio.
  • • Follow instructor guidance for safety and discipline.
  • • Use studio facilities responsibly.
  • • Support a positive and inclusive learning environment.
